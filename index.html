About Us

Sri Thirumalai Enterprises specializes in professional fencing and comprehensive landscape development services for residential, commercial, agricultural, and industrial properties.

With a strong commitment to quality materials, expert workmanship, and timely execution, we provide reliable boundary protection and visually appealing outdoor spaces. Our goal is to combine security, functionality, and design to deliver long-lasting results that meet client expectations.

Fencing Solutions

Chain Link Fencing

High-quality galvanized chain link fencing suitable for open lands, industries, schools, farms, and residential plots. Durable, cost-effective, and low maintenance.

Barbed Wire Fencing

Secure boundary protection using premium quality barbed wires. Ideal for agricultural lands, open plots, and large properties requiring economical security solutions.

Compound Wall (Razor Coil)

Heavy-duty razor coil fencing installed over compound walls for enhanced security. Suitable for factories, warehouses, commercial buildings, and high-security areas.

Agricultural & Industrial

Customized fencing solutions designed for farms, industrial zones, warehouses, and large-scale properties ensuring maximum safety and durability.
