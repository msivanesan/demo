About Us

Sri Thirumalai Enterprises specializes in professional fencing and comprehensive landscape development services for residential, commercial, agricultural, and industrial properties.

With a strong commitment to quality materials, expert workmanship, and timely execution, we provide reliable boundary protection and visually appealing outdoor spaces. Our goal is to combine security, functionality, and design to deliver long-lasting results that meet client expectations.

Our Services

Comprehensive Solutions for Your Property

Fencing Solutions

Chain Link Fencing

High-quality galvanized chain link fencing suitable for open lands, industries, schools, farms, and residential plots. Durable, cost-effective, and low maintenance.

Barbed Wire Fencing

Secure boundary protection using premium quality barbed wires. Ideal for agricultural lands, open plots, and large properties requiring economical security solutions.

Compound Wall Fencing (Razor Coil)

Heavy-duty razor coil fencing installed over compound walls for enhanced security. Suitable for factories, warehouses, commercial buildings, and high-security areas.

Agricultural & Industrial Fencing

Customized fencing solutions designed for farms, industrial zones, warehouses, and large-scale properties ensuring maximum safety and durability.

Advanced Landscape Services

Hi-Tech Green Planning

We provide modern landscape planning using advanced techniques to create sustainable, eco-friendly, and visually appealing outdoor environments. Our approach combines plant selection, layout design, and efficient resource planning for long-term greenery.

Water Body Installation

We design and construct decorative water bodies that enhance the aesthetic value of outdoor spaces, including artificial ponds and customized water features.

Statue Work (Garden Decor)

We install decorative garden statues to create elegant and artistic outdoor environments suited for residential and commercial landscapes.

Wall Statue Work

Customized wall-mounted decorative statues designed to enhance compound walls, entrance areas, and landscape focal points.

Swimming Pool Execution

We undertake complete swimming pool execution with professional planning, structural setup, and finishing for residential and commercial projects.

Wall Fountains

Installation of modern and decorative wall fountains that add a premium visual element and relaxing water effect to outdoor and indoor spaces.

Drain Cell & Zero Mat Cloth

We provide drainage solutions using drain cells and geo/zero mat cloth systems to ensure proper water flow and soil stability in landscape areas.

Water Irrigation & Automation

Installation of automated irrigation systems including drip and sprinkler setups for efficient water management and healthy plant growth.

Pergola / Steel Structure Work

We design and install pergolas and custom steel structures to enhance outdoor spaces, providing both functional shade and modern architectural appeal.

All Types of Plants Available

We supply a wide range of indoor and outdoor plants suitable for gardens, landscapes, and decorative environments.

Organic & Inorganic Manure

We provide quality organic and inorganic manure to support healthy plant growth and long-lasting landscape sustainability.
